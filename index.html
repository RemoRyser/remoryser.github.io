Welcome to Remo's homepage

This homepage aims to provide an overview of the people in the lab and the research we are conducting.

We are broadly interested in biodiversity, ecosystems and ecological networks and how they work. We primarily use theoretical tool such as maths and computer simulations to gain a general understanding of mechanisms that shape ecolocigal patterns, relationships and functions across spatial scales - fundamentally and in response to perturbations and global change.
