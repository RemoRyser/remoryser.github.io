Welcome to the ENMoLab - Ecological Networks and Modelling Lab

This homepage aims to provide an overview of the people in the lab and the research we are conducting.

We are broadly interested in biodiversity, ecosystems and ecological networks and how they work. We primarily use theoretical tool such as math and computer simulations to gain a general understanding of mechanisms that shape biodiversity and ecosystem functions - fundamentally and in response to perturbations and global change.
